Top three activities to do in Addis Ababa

Visit the national museum

The museum's most famous exhibit is the Lucy skeleton, which is the oldest hominid ever discovered and is estimated to be over three million years old.

Taste Ethiopian cuisine

Ethiopian cuisine is unique and flavorful, and Addis Ababa has some of the best restaurants to experience it.

Attend a traditional coffee ceremony

The ceremony involves roasting and brewing coffee beans, and it's often accompanied by snacks and conversation.
